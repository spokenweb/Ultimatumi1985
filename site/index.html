The Ultimatum Festival I, May 1985

The Ultimatum Montreal Urban Poetry Festival, organized by Alan Lord, was held from May 1st to 5th 1985, at the Foufounes Électriques. Ultimatum was a cutting-edge literary festival in Montreal's avant-underground scene. The 1985 Ultimatum Festival was followed by the series Les Mardis Ultimatum / Ultimatum Tuesdays held from September 23rd 1986 to May 5th 1987, and Ultimatum II in September of 1987. This project transcribes and annotates a selection of eighteen (18) reel-to-reel performance recordings from Ultimatum I.
